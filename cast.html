Cast

The movie Holes (2003), directed by Andrew Davis and based on the novel by Louis Sachar, features a strong cast. Here are the main and notable supporting cast members:
    Main Cast:
  • Shia LaBeouf as Stanley Yelnats – The protagonist who is wrongfully sent to a camp where boys dig holes.
  • Sigourney Weaver as Warden Walker – The strict and mysterious warden of Camp Green Lake.
  • Jon Voight as Mr. Sir – The camp’s tough and mean supervisor.
  • Patricia Arquette as Linda Walker – The Warden’s relative and a key figure in the story.
  • Raphael Coleman as ZigZag – A boy at the camp who has a hostile personality.
  • Khleo Thomas as Zero (Hector Zeroni) – A quiet, intelligent boy who becomes Stanley’s friend.
  • Brenden Jefferson as Twitch – A boy at Camp Green Lake who is constantly in trouble.
  • Max Casella as Barfbag – One of the boys at the camp.
  • Tim Blake Nelson as Dr. Pendanski – A counselor at Camp Green Lake who is meant to be the camp’s “nicer” authority figure.
